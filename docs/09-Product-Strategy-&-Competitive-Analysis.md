# 1. Executive Summary

PodMind AI is not being built as another AI writing tool.

Our mission is to become the world's most intelligent AI Operating System for Podcasters.

The product will combine research, planning, guest intelligence, AI writing, SEO, publishing and analytics into one unified workspace.

# 2. Vision
